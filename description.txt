Ever wanted deeper progression, more power, and real transformation growth?
Dragon Ball Ascension expands the Terraria experience with a full Ki, transformation, and mastery system inspired by legendary warriors.

Features

• Multiple hidden Traits with unique bonuses, strengths, and drawbacks
• Trait reroll system available later in progression
• Selectable Races with different affinities and transformation paths
• Extensive Transformation system with progression, mastery, and advanced forms
• Mentor NPCs who guide your growth and unlock new power
• Fusion system designed for safe single-player and multiplayer use
• Hakai — wield destructive energy, or suffer it if you push too far
• Ki resource system with regeneration, drain, and upgrades
• Form mastery and advanced evolution mechanics
• More features planned as development continues

Power grows. Mastery decides everything.
